MAX Book Bytes

About

I recently got a job recently as a research software engineer in Portland. I ride the MAX for around 2 hours every day and I have been using that time to read. Recently I realized that I should probably write about the books that I read since I also enjoy writing and I don't do it much anymore. For each book review I will write a paragraph and then take a photo of what the book represents to me as part of a "qualitative" rating. Excuse my CSS skills they are very mediocre, I am not a front end web developer, or not right now at least.

I plan to keep this reading habit for as long as I am riding the MAX and who knows maybe even longer if I can keep finding interesting books to read. I find value in reading the thoughts of those who have come before and those who are still living today.

I also just got into photography recently. I'm not very good at it, but like anything in life a little bit of practice can go a long way. And a lot of practice can go even further. It's fun and I figured I would fuse software, reading, writing, and photography together into this website since I am passionate in all of those hobbies. If you are interested in any of the books the book name title of each book review is a direct link to the book's Amazon listing. So enjoy this random leaf.
